Our Services

1. U.S. Education — Comparative Analysis

Macro analysis of US K12 & higher education vs. mainland China; system differences and outcomes.

2. College Application Overview

Overview of mainstream universities, application process, curricula, platforms and tuition; consulting & resources.

3. Pre-College Profile (AP / Summer)

Course selection, AP planning, summer programs and extracurricular strategy to strengthen profile.

4. Financial Aid (FAFSA / CSS) — One-on-One

Detailed FAFSA & CSS walkthroughs, grant/loan strategies, and institutional aid support.

5. Scholarship Application Support

Merit & need-based scholarship search, essay strategy and application management for HS and college.

6. Family & Long-term Education Planning

Holistic K12 → college planning to maximize outcomes across siblings and family situations.

7. Education Savings & Tax (ESA / 529)

Account selection, tax-efficient structures, and cross-jurisdiction considerations for education savings.

8. College Fund Design & Financial Strategy

Design long-term college funding strategies, investment allocation, and risk planning aligned to family goals.

9. Family Foundation / 501(c)(3) Setup

Establish compliant charitable entities: governance, filing, tax rules and operational considerations.

10. Education + Wealth & Trust Planning

Trust design and intergenerational planning that coordinates education goals with wealth preservation strategies.

11. Student Housing & Nearby Real Estate Services

Advisory & execution for student housing and nearby property investment to ensure quality living and optimal returns.
