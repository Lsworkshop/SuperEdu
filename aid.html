Helping Families Optimize Financial Aid & Scholarship Opportunities

We provide complimentary guidance on maximizing financial aid and scholarship opportunities through education accounts, tax-efficient strategies, and family asset planning. Financial Aid Estimator Overview

Our Financial Aid Estimator is designed to simulate common U.S. college financial aid scenarios by evaluating family affordability and estimating potential Financial Aid ranges.

🎓

For Undergraduate Students

Financial aid eligibility is commonly evaluated based on parent and student income, net assets, and overall family financial capacity. Income generally has a greater impact, while certain assets may not be included in calculations.

🏛️

For Graduate & Doctoral Programs

Graduate, doctoral, and specialized research programs often follow different financial aid methodologies. Please contact us or related nonprofit organizations for customized evaluation strategies.

💛

Edunova Need-Based Scholarship & Aid

Edunova Foundation provides special scholarships and financial assistance for families facing financial hardship or significant tuition burdens, helping students continue their education journey.

How to Maximize Financial Aid & Scholarships

Contact us for complimentary guidance on financial aid strategies, scholarship opportunities, education accounts, and tax-efficient planning solutions.

📄

Income Impact

The prior-year IRS Form 1040 and MAGI (Modified Adjusted Gross Income) are among the most important factors in financial aid calculations.

🎒

Asset Impact

Assets generally have a smaller impact than income in financial aid formulas, and certain qualified assets may not be included in the evaluation.
