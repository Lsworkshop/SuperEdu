Volunteer with Edunova Foundation

Empowering global education through student-led volunteer initiatives.

Who We Are

Edunova Foundation is a registered Non-Profit Organization (501(c)(3)) dedicated to expanding access to quality education for students worldwide.

Our volunteer programs are primarily led and organized by motivated High School students.

What Our Volunteers Do

Online Education

Volunteers provide free online courses and lectures worldwide.

Community Support

Support global learning communities.

Limited In-Person Activities

Selected in-person activities with High Schools.

Certificates & Recognition

Based on verified volunteer service hours, Edunova Foundation awards official certificates.

Volunteer Certificate

Official recognition of verified service hours.

Outstanding Volunteer Award

Granted to volunteers with exceptional contribution.

Award Plus

Extended leadership and long-term impact recognition.

These recognitions are highly valuable for college applications and scholarships.

Certificate System Overview

Edunova Foundation issues volunteer certificates based on verified service hours, level of responsibility, and demonstrated impact.

All recognitions emphasize authenticity, long-term engagement, and educational value rather than short-term participation.

Volunteer Certificate

Issued to volunteers who have completed verified service hours.

This certificate documents consistent participation.

Outstanding Volunteer Award

Awarded to volunteers with exceptional dedication.

Selection is based on qualitative evaluation.

Award Plus

Award Plus recognizes long-term commitment and leadership.

This recognition reflects responsibility and sustained impact.
